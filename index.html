Method

One model. Every workflow.

Stride's data model unifies issues, projects, cycles, and roadmaps into a single graph. Filter, slice, and reshape your work without losing context — your team stays in flow while the system handles the bookkeeping.

  • Real-time sync — no save buttons, no conflicts
  • Keyboard-first interface, palette-driven actions
  • Native git, calendar, and chat integrations
  • Offline-capable with conflict-free merges
